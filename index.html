Image Search Abstraction Layer

url: https://my-image-search-abstraction-layer.glitch.me/api/imagesearch/

Example: https://my-image-search-abstraction-layer.glitch.me/api/imagesearch/european union?offset=10

Features:

  • paginate through the responses by adding a ?offset=2 parameter to the URL.
  • get a list of the most recently submitted search strings.
